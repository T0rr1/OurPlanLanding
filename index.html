How It Works

One setup.
Runs every month.

OurPlan isn't something you maintain — it's a system that holds your financial life together quietly, week after week.

01

Set your plan

Enter combined income, choose your Essentials / Growth / Reserves / Flex split. Done in under 5 minutes.

02

Autopilot guides you

Payday lands. OurPlan notifies you. A short checklist helps both partners confirm every move is covered.

03

Log & sync live

Either partner adds a transaction — the other sees it instantly. No "did you spend that?" — the plan always knows.

04

10-min check-in

Sunday evening. Guided Money Meeting. Review the week, celebrate wins, set intentions. Then forget about money until next Sunday.

Features

Built for two.
Not retrofitted.

Most budgeting apps are solo tools awkwardly shared. OurPlan was designed from day one around a couple — two people, one plan.

Essentials, Growth, Reserves, Flex

Four proven budget categories that cover everything. Allocate your combined income once — OurPlan handles the math and keeps both of you on the same page.

Autopilot Tab

Set up autopay, scheduled transfers, and investment contributions with a clear checklist. OurPlan shows which accounts to create and what to automate so payday becomes repeatable.

Weekly Check-in

A guided 10-minute Money Meeting every week. Built-in agenda so you never run out of things to say. Review spending, celebrate wins, align on the week ahead.

Shared Goals

A house, a trip, an emergency fund — set targets together, watch the number grow together. Goals you share are goals you actually hit.

Insights & What-If

Spot patterns, track budget drift, and pressure-test changes before you make them. What if one of us gets a raise? What if we cut dining out? See it first.

Live iCloud Sync

One partner logs a transaction — the other sees it immediately. No refresh, no "hey did you add that?" — your plan is always current for both of you.
