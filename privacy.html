1. Overview

Short version: OurPlan stores your data in your own iCloud account. We do not sell your data, we do not share it with advertisers, and we do not run third-party analytics on your financial information.

OurPlan ("we", "our", "us") is a couples budgeting application for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use OurPlan, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your information.

By using OurPlan, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Data We Collect

OurPlan collects only the information you explicitly provide to run the budgeting features of the app.

Financial data you enter

  • Monthly income amounts for each partner
  • Budget bucket names, categories, and allocated amounts
  • Transactions — amounts, dates, notes, categories, merchant names
  • Savings goals — names, target amounts, deadlines, progress
  • Money Meeting responses and notes

Partner information

  • First names used to identify partners within the app (e.g. "Alex" and "Jordan")
  • A randomly generated invite code used to link two partners — this is ephemeral and discarded after pairing

App preferences

  • Display settings (light/dark mode preference)
  • Notification preferences (payday reminders, weekly meeting alerts)
  • Payday schedule and cadence

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All budget and transaction data is stored in two places:

Storage locationWhat lives thereWho controls it
On-device (SwiftData) All app data — buckets, transactions, goals, meetings You
iCloud (CloudKit) Synced copy of your plan, used to share with your partner Apple / You

Sync between partners is handled entirely through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. OurPlan does not operate its own servers for user data. Your data travels directly between your device and your iCloud account, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

The invite link used to pair partners passes through a CloudKit public database record, which is deleted after the pairing is complete or after 48 hours, whichever comes first.

4. Third Parties

OurPlan does not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information with any third party for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

Apple services used

  • iCloud / CloudKit — partner data sync
  • StoreKit 2 — in-app subscription purchases (Apple processes all payment information; OurPlan never sees your payment details)
  • UserNotifications — local notifications scheduled entirely on-device, no data leaves your phone

All Apple services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

OurPlan offers an optional Pro subscription (monthly and annual) processed entirely through Apple's App Store via StoreKit 2.

  • OurPlan never receives, stores, or processes your payment card details
  • Subscription status is verified locally on your device using StoreKit 2's local transaction verification — no data is sent to OurPlan's servers
  • To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone

6. Children's Privacy

OurPlan is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through our app, please contact us at support@getourplan.app and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Because your data lives in your iCloud account and on your device, you have full control at all times.

Delete your data

  • Delete all data from the app: Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All Data inside OurPlan. This removes all local SwiftData records immediately.
  • Remove iCloud data: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → OurPlan → Delete Data
  • Uninstall the app: Deleting OurPlan from your device removes all locally stored data automatically.

Data portability

OurPlan Pro includes a CSV export feature that lets you download all your transaction and budget data in a standard format at any time.

California residents (CCPA)

Because OurPlan does not sell your personal information to third parties, most CCPA sale opt-out rights do not apply. You still have the right to know what data is collected (described above) and to delete it (instructions above). Contact us at support@getourplan.app with any requests.

EU/UK residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, your data is processed under the lawful basis of contract (to provide the app service you requested). You have the right to access, correct, and erase your data. Contact support@getourplan.app to exercise these rights.
