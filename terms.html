2. Description of Service

OurPlan is a personal finance application designed for couples to collaboratively manage a shared budget. The App provides tools for:

  • Creating and managing shared budget buckets
  • Logging and categorizing financial transactions
  • Conducting guided weekly money check-ins ("Money Meetings")
  • Setting and tracking shared savings goals
  • Synchronizing financial data between two partners via iCloud
  • Viewing spending insights, trends, and hypothetical scenarios (Pro tier)

4. Account & Partner Access

OurPlan does not require a traditional account with an email and password. Access is tied to your Apple ID and iCloud account.

Partner linking

One partner ("Partner A") initiates the shared plan and generates an invite link or code. The second partner ("Partner B") joins by opening that link. Once linked, both partners share access to the same budget data via iCloud.

Your responsibilities

  • You are responsible for maintaining the security of your Apple ID
  • You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your iCloud account within the App
  • Only share your invite code with the person you intend to be your budget partner
  • You are responsible for ensuring your partner's access is appropriate at all times

Partner data access

When you link with a partner, both parties gain read and write access to the shared budget data. You should only link with a partner you trust with full access to your shared financial plan.
